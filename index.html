Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about teaching in China and working with EduExploreChina.

What qualifications do I need to teach in China?

Most schools require a bachelor’s degree and a TEFL certification. Some positions may require prior teaching experience.

How long does the visa process take?

The visa process typically takes 4–6 weeks. We assist you at every stage to make it as efficient and smooth as possible.

Will I get help finding housing?

Yes! Most schools offer housing or provide support in finding a comfortable, nearby apartment for teachers.

Do I need to speak Chinese?

Not necessarily. Many teaching positions are in English-speaking environments, and basic survival Chinese is enough to get by.

What kind of support will I receive after placement?

We provide ongoing support throughout your time in China—including regular check-ins, community events, and professional development.
